Job Description

Location: Warsaw or Poznań

Why you should join us

  • We use the most innovative technologies (e.g. Kotlin or Rx Java)
  • Our Android app has over 5 million monthly active users, including 2 million unique ones
  • Code review, Continuous Integration, Scrum/Kanban (depending on a team), Pair Programming, Test Lab / Device Farm
  • Join a team of 600 excellent software engineers who have shared their experience during hundreds of tech conferences such as Devoxx PL, GeeCon PL and Prague, Confitura, Agile By Example, Agile India 2016 (Bangalore), Mesos Con (Denver), Atmosphere and contribute to the allegro.tech blog
  • Every year, we send over 100 software engineers to participate in conferences such as Google I/O, DroidCon, AnDevCon and other events that take place in Poland and abroad; each team has budget for training and books, so if you want to improve your skills, we will support you
  • We offer laptops with i7, 16GB RAM and SSD drives, e.g. MacBook Pro 15” or 13” or similar Dell devices running on Ubuntu/Windows if you are not a Mac fan, two monitors and all the gadgets you need
  • Internal ecosystem based on self-service and popular tools such as Marathon, Docker, Consul, Bitbucket or Bamboo. Therefore, from day one, you can start working on software whose language, architecture or scale is limited only by your own imagination
  • We actively contribute to Polish tech communities (Java, Python, Devops) by organizing training sessions/workshops and giving lectures in Poznań, Warsaw, Toruń and Kraków.
  • Technological autonomy – choose the best technology to address a given issue without going through needless formalities. Just remember that you are responsible for your solution.
  • 10 thousand servers grouped in two data centers
  • 40 open source projects, 3391 github stars

What we offer

  • Startup-like working culture and stability of a mature organization
  • Modern office and tools
  • Friendly working environment in a professional team
  • Excellent choice of benefits provided in addition to remuneration
  • Free English classes adjusted to the needs of software engineers

Skills & Requirements

We are looking for software engineers who:

  • are expert Android users and are experienced in working with Android development tools and Google Play mechanisms
  • have strong Java skills, which are essential; Kotlin skills are „nice to have”
  • are familiar with issues concerning designing, object-oriented programming, algorithms and data structure
  • use Android Studio or IntelliJ IDEA, and version control systems
  • want to be proud of code they create, are open to feedback, and share knowledge during peer code reviews
  • can provide effective solutions to answer users’ needs
  • want to learn and improve their skills

About Allegro

At Allegro, we have been shaping online shopping habits of Polish users since 1999 with one goal in mind – to make shopping and selling online a joyful experience.

We are one of the largest technology companies in the CEE region. What we do goes beyond impressive sale statistics and involves technological and business challenges making Allegro a unique place where 1,400 employees can gain experience and improve their skills. Nearly half of our crew works in the technology area and is responsible for platform development and maintenance.
